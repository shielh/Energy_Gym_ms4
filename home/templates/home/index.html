About

Our Story

Energy Gym was founded in 2021 and is a family run gym. We didn't want to be another gym that people join on January 1st and then forget about it a week later. We wanted Energy Gym to be a place where our members feel comfortable and empowered to work at their own pace to achieve their own personal goals. Since opening our doors on March 13th of 2021, we have 86 regular members. We want to keep our gym feeling like a community where everyone can get to know everyone.

What we do

We want to help you live a fit and healthy lifestyle! We do this by helping you find the most suitable program, keeping your budget, lifestyle and fitness goals in mind. We offer a wide range of membership options, with class bundles as an option too. Our class timetable will be added to our site soon so stay tuned! But for now, feel free to pop in to say hello and we will give you a run through of everything we offer. Our awesome team is always keen to help, so please call us to discuss your needs.
